Your 12-string acoustic is really nice-- tell me about it.

It's a Guild JF-65 with lots of abalone, a gorgeous maple body & a Martin piezo pickup. It's the best acoustic 12-string I've ever seen or played-- a work of art. Occasionally I'll play Hal's Ovation or John's Takamine classical, but this is my favorite.

How do you play acoustic live during an electric set?

With a Gracie stand that clamps on & holds it in position. If we were playing acoustic exclusively I'd prefer to sit though.
