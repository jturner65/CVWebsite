So, Hal, what was your childhood like?

You don't want to know. Honest. After the 10 years of forced labor in a Russian Prison camp, I wrote my 2nd treatise on the perils of neo-socialist economic policies in a democratic republic, only to see my works decried as Objectivist Neo-Fascism. Combined with my budding career as a professional bowler, cut short by a tragic thumb injury, my youth was hard. Very hard. Sometimes I wonder how I made it.
