Hal Chiasson on Lead Guitar
and Vocals
Synthesizers and Guitar Synth

 

What kind of synth setup do you use?

My main synthesizer is an Alesis Quadrasynth as a brain and as a controller, and for many of the solos I use my Yamaha AN1X - I love this little piece. I use a Roland JV-1010 rack-unit, as well as a Peavey unit for organ and synth sounds.

How do you approach your keyboard parts? Do they support the guitar, vice versa or neither?

I sneak up on them under cover of darkness. [laughs] I treat them like any other of my parts-- it's what I hear in my head that ends up working. The only hard part is getting it from my head to my fingers. In the end, though, I don't really think about it too much - I try to just let it flow.

What about your Guitar synths - what exactly is going on there?

I have a Roland VG-8 and Roland GR-1, and I also have a special A/B switch where I can send the same signal to either of the 2, or to both of them, from any of my synth pickup-equipped guitars. I then use a line mixer to combine the two signals, along with my various synthesizers, and my straight guitar from the Line 6 AX 212.
